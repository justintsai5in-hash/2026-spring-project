Methodology

Study Design

We watch the documentary of Twelve Nights, we think 12 night video and design the survey, going to the shelter. We go back the school, Write fish bone, 5whys, find action point.Finish after we begin think question,first we do online research,putting all the questions together, discussing questions with instructor, finally revise the question with instructor advice.

Participants

We ask 50 people, 4 students 46 stangers.

Materials/Tools

Go to ask people before, we need ready question list/ microphone/ camera/ stationery/ poster for questions and choices.

Procedure

We finally go out sind to ask people, we have a speaker: Catch people and tell them what we are doing. Tell them the question/ Recorder: Write down the answer and 2 Helper: Hold the things to show the options of the questions.OMG I see person, We process survey is Introduce ourselves and our project/ Collecting data with face-to-face means and ask the question before asking their age.

Special Considerations

Giving the survey need speak loudly, find a good place, don’t ask more than one person at the same time,consistent, don’t change the introduction, don’t switch job, be respectful, don’t say bad word, don’t sit down, don’t chase the people, avoid bias.

Data Analysis

We need make processing and analyzing.Processing the data: (Steps that organize the data so it is easy to read and see patterns).Compile the answers/ Sort the data and visualize data.Analyzing the data: (Explanation of important things we see when we look at the data). Describe the data (results are region specific), conclude the data.
