Producion Journal

Justin:act bed owner

We made repeated attempts to film the scene where my friend Afle hits me—a scene triggered by my dog ​​relieving itself all over the place and my having to borrow money for its medical treatment. The shoot was a struggle; although I was supposed to groan and look pained, I couldn't bring myself to make a sound, and instead, a smile kept involuntarily spreading across my face. In the end, something about the final footage just didn't feel quite right.

For this scene, I needed to look like I was crying, but since I couldn't actually shed tears, I had to use eye drops instead. During the first take, I kept blurting out "Oh..."—a verbal tic of mine—but fortunately, I managed to break the habit by the end.
